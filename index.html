Instructions

Step 1


Cut along lines shown in orange and remove mask material. Make sure to leave a little extra under the nose bar to ensure it doesnt come undone.

Step 2

Fold the nose bar exactly halfway. Place the nose bar over your face ensuring the center point is on the bridge of your nose. Use a pencil to mark where the nose meets the cheek.

Step 3

Using the lines that you have drawn, loop the elastic bands over the nose bar. Position the chin strap as shown. Tie the ends of the ear loops loosely around both rubber bands as shown. Repeat for both sides.

Step 4

Place the mask over your face and the chin elastic below the chin. Adjust the fit of the mask so both elastic bands are flat against the face. When you are happy with the fit, tie a double knot to permanently secure the ear loops, cutting away any excess.
